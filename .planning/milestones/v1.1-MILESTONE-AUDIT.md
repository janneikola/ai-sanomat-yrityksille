## Cross-Phase Integration

### Integration Issues

#### INT-01: Health Status Key Mismatch (Visual Bug)

**From:** Phase 5 `sourceHealthService.computeHealthStatus()` returns `green | yellow | red`
**To:** Phase 9 `x-monitoring/page.tsx` `healthDot()` expects `healthy | degraded | failing`
**Impact:** Health dots on X monitoring page render as gray fallback with raw status text
**Affected Requirements:** HEALTH-03, SRC-06
**Fix:** Change `healthDot()` keys in `x-monitoring/page.tsx` lines 117-125 to `green/yellow/red`

#### INT-02: Duplicate Items Not Filtered in Digest Generation (Functional Gap)

**From:** Phase 8 `deduplicationService` flags items with `isDuplicate: true`
**To:** Phase 2 `newsletterService.generateClientDigest()` queries news items without filtering `isDuplicate`
**Impact:** Flagged duplicates may appear in generated digests, causing redundant newsletter content
**Affected Requirements:** DEDUP-03
**Fix:** Add `.where(eq(newsItems.isDuplicate, false))` to news query in `newsletterService.generateClientDigest()`
